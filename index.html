Conference Management System
NIT Andhra Pradesh.

Conference Management System is a program for students to attend conferences and for speakers to collect submissions and create a schedule.

  Become a member

Concept of this

The program will help students to attend their conference according to a fixed schedule and timing will be marked accordingly.

Why Conference Management?

For students to make their life easier, solving our problems with the use of technology.

Target audience

This program is targeted at University students and any others including member who want to have online conference experience or want to conduct a conference.

User applications

  • Schedule a conference as a speaker and send RSVP to the students, all the details can be mentioned in it.
  • Student can register and attend a conference, add a conference to their schedule and manage it.
  • Speaker can manage the timing and detials of the conference and check the registrations.
  • Students and speakers can login or register for the program and continue availing the services offered.
